What is the difference between arabica and robusta coffee beans?

Once roasted, pretty much all coffee beans look the same. But that there are actually dozens of different varieties of coffee beans: arabica and robusta. These are the two primary types of coffee cultivated for drinking.

ARABICA ROBUSTA
Look
Larger and more oval with a curved centre crease More circular and thicker, with straighter line down the midle
Taste
Vibrant, complex and sweet Strong, bitter, harsh and deep
Price
2 x more than robusta Less expensive, lower quality
Traits
Difficult to farm, 1/2 caffeine of robusta Easy to farm, high in caffeine
Location
Brazil is the largest producing country Vietnam is the largest producing country
