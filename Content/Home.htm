A few words about coffee

Behind only petroleum, coffee is the most valuable legally traded commodity in the world. People love it and drink it in massive quantities.

Arabica vs. robusta

Once roasted, pretty much all coffee beans look the same. But that there are actually dozens of different varieties of coffee beans: arabica and robusta. These are the two primary types of coffee cultivated for drinking.

Difference between espresso and brewed coffee

Espresso is not a type of bean or strength of coffee. Espresso is a method of brewing, and it requires ~9 bars of pressure to process.

Coffee and spices

Adding a pinch of spices to your coffee can take a pot of coffee from bland to share-worthy. You can choose from flavor-enhancing options like cinnamon, cocoa, cardamom, and more.
